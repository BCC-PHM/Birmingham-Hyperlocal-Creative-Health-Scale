# Birmingham Hyperlocal Creative Health Scale 

The Birmingham Creative Health Scale 2026 is an interactive map and database of Birmingham’s 69 electoral wards. It brings together 16 independent datasets, across 4 creative health domains, to enable policymakers, leaders and artists to better allocate time and resources for improving the overall wellbeing and creative participation rates of the city’s residents. The 4 domains are Adolescents, Children & Young Adults, Cultural Infrastructure, Environmental Challenges and Socioeconomic Inequalities. The Birmingham Hyperlocal Creative Health Scale 2026 presents 21 individual maps consisting of 16 maps for the independent datasets, 4 maps for the 4 domains (based on the average tier for each dataset), and a final ACES map of the overall rankings for the 4 domains combined. This collaboration between Birmingham City Council and Birmingham Hippodrome is the first iteration of the scale, and it will continue to develop and improve as new datasets become available.
